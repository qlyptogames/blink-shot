Account Deletion

Understand what data is removed and easily request your account deletion below.

What data is deleted

When you trigger an account deletion request, the following information is permanently and irreversibly removed from our Firebase servers:

  • Authentication Record: Your Firebase login identifiers (Google or Apple sign-in linking tokens).
  • User Profile: Your display name, character customize configurations, and cosmetic options in Firestore.
  • Match History & Progress: High scores, game records, prestige levels, coin totals, wins, and losses.

Data Retention Policy

We believe in strict data protection principles. We keep your data only as long as necessary, enforcing these automated timelines:

  • Permanent Accounts: Accounts linked to Google/Apple credentials submitted via email request are fully purged within 48 hours.
  • Anonymous/Guest Accounts: Local progress profiles are automatically wiped from our server database after 30 days of inactivity.

Choose Deletion Method

You can request deletion directly within the app, or generate an email request below.

  • 1
    Open App Settings
    Launch Blink Out on your mobile device and navigate to the settings menu icon from the main screen lobby.
  • 2
    Tap "Delete Account"
    Locate the dedicated red button in the settings panel marked "Delete Account".
  • 3
    Confirm Action
    Confirm the alert dialog popup. Your profile and game progress will be immediately and permanently cleared.
