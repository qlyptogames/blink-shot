Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2, 2026

Welcome to Blink Out. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you play our game.

1 Data Collection & Use

A. Camera Data & Face Detection

Blink Out uses the camera on your device to perform real-time face detection and eye-tracking for gameplay (detecting blinks).

  • Local Processing: All image and facial mesh data is processed locally on your device using on-device Machine Learning (ML Kit).
  • No Storage: We do not record, store, or transmit raw camera feeds or facial images to our servers or any third parties.
  • Purpose: This data is used solely to determine match outcomes within the active game session.

B. Account & Profile Data

If you sign in using a social account (Google or Apple), we collect:

  • Identity: Your unique User ID and Display Name.
  • Game Progress: Your prestige, coins (score), match history, wins, and losses.
  • Customization: Your mascot color preference and theme settings.

This data is stored securely in Firebase to provide cross-device persistence and leaderboard functionality.

2 Third-Party Services

We utilize the following third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, cloud database (Firestore/Realtime Database), and analytics.
  • WebRTC: For real-time multiplayer signaling (P2P). No data from these streams is recorded by us.
  • Google Fonts: For typography rendering.

3 Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures provided by Firebase to protect your progress and profile data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

4 User Choices

  • Anonymous Play: You can play Blink Out anonymously without signing in. Note that progress will be lost if you delete the app or change devices.
  • Data Deletion: You may request the deletion of your account and associated progress data at any time directly through the app's settings menu or through our deletion request page.

    Looking to delete your account and remove all personal information? You can do this directly within the game, or submit a request on our dedicated page.

5 Children's Privacy

Blink Out is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

6 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
